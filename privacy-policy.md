# Privacy Policy for RealtyCalc
Last updated: 12/9/2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
RealtyCalc is designed to operate with minimal data collection. We may collect:

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- Email address (if applicable in future updates)
- Optional saved scenarios or app settings stored on your device

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- Device type and operating system  
- Crash data for debugging and performance improvement  

We DO NOT collect:
- Personal financial data  
- Sensitive personal information  
- Contact lists  
- Location data

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We may use collected information to:
- Improve app performance and user experience  
- Fix bugs and optimize features  
- Provide customer support  
- Maintain app security and reliability  

We do NOT sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services
RealtyCalc may use trusted third-party tools such as:

These services may collect anonymous technical data per their own privacy policies.

## 4. Data Storage
Most data generated by the App is stored:
- Locally on your device, or  
- Within Apple’s secure infrastructure if using iCloud or App Store–related services  

We do not store or manage your personal financial calculations on external servers.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
RealtyCalc is not intended for children under 13.  
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
